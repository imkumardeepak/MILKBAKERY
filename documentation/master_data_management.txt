MASTER DATA MANAGEMENT

The Milk & Bakery Management System provides comprehensive master data management capabilities across multiple business entities:

1. Customer Management:
- Customer_Master entity stores detailed customer information
- Fields include: name, contact person, division, account type, route, address, phone, email
- Sequence field for ordering
- Related to DealerMaster through DistributorId
- Supports customer segmentation through CustomerSegementMap

2. Dealer Management:
- DealerMaster entity for distributor information
- Includes: name, route code, address, city, phone, email
- Related to customers through DistributorId
- Supports basic order creation with DealerBasicOrder

3. Employee Management:
- EmployeeMaster stores employee details
- Fields: name, phone number, designation, department, grade
- Related to customer mapping through EmpToCustMap

4. Product/ Material Management:
- MaterialMaster entity for product information
- Fields: material name, short name, unit, category, subcategory
- Price, sequence, segment name, 3rd party code
- Active status flag
- Crate type association

5. Company and Segment Management:
- CompanyMaster for company information
- SegementMaster for business segments
- Company_SegementMap for company-segment relationships
- CustomerSegementMap for customer-segment relationships

6. Route Management:
- RouteMaster for delivery route information
- Connected to customers through route field in Customer_Master

7. Category Management:
- CategoryMaster for product categories
- Sub_CategoryMaster for sub-categories
- UnitMaster for units of measure
- DepartmentMaster, DesignationMaster, GradeMaster for organizational data

8. Plant Management:
- PlantMaster for plant location information

9. Crates Management:
- CratesType for different crate types
- Related to CratesManage records

10. User Management:
- User entity for system users
- Role entity for user roles
- PageAccess and MenuItem for menu/navigation control

11. Mapping Entities:
- Cust2CustMap for customer-to-customer relationships
- EmpToCustMap for employee-to-customer relationships

Key Features:
- CRUD operations for all master entities
- Role-based access control
- Data validation through model annotations
- Relationship management between entities
- Search and filtering capabilities
- Integration with order processing and reporting
