MILK & BAKERY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM - TECHNICAL DOCUMENTATION

Project Overview:
The Milk & Bakery Management System is an enterprise-level application designed for dairy and bakery businesses to streamline daily operations, improve data accuracy, and enhance decision-making through comprehensive reporting.

For developers, administrators, and users who need to understand the system's functionality, architecture, and operation procedures.
